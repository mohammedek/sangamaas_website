First of Its Kind CSR Initiative

Now, Over 12 years in the field of NGO Consulting, SANGAMAAS INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION Proudly presents WEMAAA – 2022 Chennai on 19th July 2022 “Connect – Associate - Integrate” first of its Kind association platform for NGOs and Corporates to Analyse and connect on National and International Wellness & Environmental CSR Programs and Projects. WEMAAA provides a wonderful opportunity for Individuals, Corporates, and NGOs to get Associated for the Better World. WEMAAA is certainly a Transformation Initiative for the World of Wellness and create Dynamic Equilibrium on Environmental Segments. The Global Scientists and Researchers Have predicted that the next 18 to 20 years will be the most Dynamic Era that the Humans have ever seen. Transforming Ideas Has Emerged in almost all segments and WEMAA Invites Individuals, Entrepreneurs, Corporates and NGOs to this Global Movement for World of Wellness and Environmental Transformation..
